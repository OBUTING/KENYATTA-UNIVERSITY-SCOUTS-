About Us

The Kenyatta University Scouts Club is a standout student organization at Kenyatta University, widely recognized as one of the institution's top clubs due to its impressive track record in competitions and its vibrant contribution to campus life. Rooted in the principles of the Kenya Scouts Association, the club embodies values such as teamwork, leadership, discipline, and community service, making it a dynamic platform for students to develop both personally and socially.

The club has distinguished itself by consistently excelling in various scouting competitions, earning accolades at regional and national levels. These victories highlight the members' mastery of scouting skills, including outdoor survival techniques, first aid, navigation, and community-oriented projects. Their success in competition camps, which often assess participants on inspections, practical bases, and innovative projects, showcases their ability to work effectively in patrols and tackle real-world challenges with creativity and commitment. This competitive edge is a testament to the club's rigorous training and the dedication of its members, who range from junior to senior levels, fostering a strong sense of camaraderie and skill progression.

Beyond its competitive achievements, the Kenyatta University Scouts Club is celebrated for its role in enhancing the university experience. It offers students opportunities to engage in adventure-based activities, interact with peers from diverse backgrounds, and contribute to societal solutions—values aligned with the global scouting movement founded by Lord Baden-Powell. The club's prominence is further underscored by its status as a leading organization within Kenyatta University’s Directorate of Student Affairs, where it stands out among both academic and social clubs for its blend of practical skill-building and community impact. For students seeking personal growth, leadership development, and a chance to shine in a competitive arena, the Scouts Club remains a premier choice at Kenyatta University.

Our Vision

To inspire and develop young individuals into responsible, confident, and compassionate leaders who contribute positively to their communities and the world.

Our Mission

To provide a dynamic and inclusive environment that fosters personal growth, teamwork, and life skills through scouting activities, outdoor adventures, and community service.

Our Programs

Outdoor Adventures

Engaging activities like camping, hiking, and survival skills training to build resilience, teamwork, and a love for nature.

Community Service

Volunteer projects and initiatives that teach scouts the value of giving back and making a difference in their communities.

Leadership Development

Workshops and activities designed to enhance leadership skills, decision-making, and confidence in young scouts.
